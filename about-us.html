About Prime Tools

India's free financial calculator platform — built to make finance simple for everyone

Who We Are

Making finance simple for every Indian

Prime Tools is a free financial tools platform built specifically for Indian users. We believe that every person — whether a salaried employee, a business owner, a student, or a homemaker — deserves access to accurate financial calculators without any cost or complexity.

We started with a simple question: why should someone pay or sign up just to calculate their EMI, check their GST, or plan their SIP investments? The answer is they shouldn't. That's why every tool on Prime Tools is completely free, works instantly in your browser, and requires zero login or registration.

Our Mission

Financial clarity for 1.4 billion Indians

India has one of the world's most complex tax systems — GST, income tax slabs, HRA exemptions, TDS, and more. Add to that investment decisions around SIP, FD, PPF, and loan EMIs. Most people either struggle with spreadsheets or pay a CA for basic calculations.

Our mission is to change that. We are building the most comprehensive, accurate, and easy-to-use financial tools platform in India — completely free, forever.

The Team

Built by an Indian entrepreneur

Prime Tools is an independent project built and maintained by a solo founder based in India. This platform was created out of a genuine need — the frustration of not finding a single, reliable, India-specific financial calculator that works without ads, login walls, or confusing UI.

We are a lean, bootstrapped operation with zero external funding. Every tool is built from scratch with care, accuracy and the Indian user in mind. We plan to grow this into India's most comprehensive free finance platform over the next 2 years.
