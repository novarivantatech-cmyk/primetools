About Tipping in India

Tipping is a way to show appreciation for good service at restaurants, hotels, spas, and for delivery personnel. While tipping is not mandatory in India, it is a kind gesture that motivates service staff. Our Tip Calculator makes it easy to calculate the right tip and split it between friends.

How Much to Tip in India

  • Restaurants: 10% to 15% of the bill for good service
  • Fine Dining: 15% to 20% for excellent service
  • Food Delivery: Rs 20 to Rs 50 per delivery
  • Hotel Staff: Rs 50 to Rs 200 per service
  • Cab/Auto: Round up the fare or Rs 20 to Rs 50 extra
  • Salon/Spa: 10% of the service bill

Frequently Asked Questions

How to calculate 10% tip on a restaurant bill?

To calculate 10% tip, simply divide the bill by 10. Example: Bill is Rs 1,500. Tip = Rs 1,500 / 10 = Rs 150. Total = Rs 1,650. For 15% tip, find 10% and add half of that. Rs 150 + Rs 75 = Rs 225 tip.

Should I tip on the pre-tax or post-tax amount?

In India, it is common to tip on the total bill amount including GST. However, some people prefer to tip on the food amount before GST. Either way is acceptable — what matters is showing appreciation for good service.
