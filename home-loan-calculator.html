Home Loan Guide for India 2026

A home loan or housing loan is a secured loan given by banks and financial institutions to help you purchase a residential property. In India, home loans are available at interest rates starting from 8.35% per annum. The loan tenure can be up to 30 years and banks typically finance up to 80% of the property value.

Current Home Loan Rates 2026

SBI home loan rate starts at 8.50%, HDFC Bank at 8.60%, ICICI Bank at 8.75%, Axis Bank at 8.75%, and Kotak Mahindra Bank at 8.65%. Women borrowers get a 0.05% concession on home loan rates from most banks.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the EMI for Rs 30 lakh home loan for 20 years?

At 8.5% interest rate for 20 years, the EMI for a Rs 30 lakh home loan is approximately Rs 26,035 per month. Total payment = Rs 62.48 lakh, Total interest = Rs 32.48 lakh. Use our calculator above to calculate for your specific loan amount.

How much down payment is required for a home loan?

RBI mandates that banks can lend a maximum of 80% of property value for loans above Rs 30 lakh. So minimum down payment is 20% of property value. For a Rs 50 lakh property, minimum down payment = Rs 10 lakh.

Is home loan interest tax deductible in India?

Yes. Under Section 24(b) of Income Tax Act, home loan interest up to Rs 2 lakh per year is deductible for self-occupied property under the old tax regime. Principal repayment up to Rs 1.5 lakh qualifies for Section 80C deduction.

Should I prepay my home loan?

Prepaying your home loan in the early years saves maximum interest. Even a single extra EMI per year can reduce your loan tenure by 3 to 4 years. Use our Prepayment Calculator tab above to see exactly how much you save with prepayment.
