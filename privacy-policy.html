Privacy Policy

How we collect, use and protect your information

Last updated: June 2026 | Effective date: June 2026
Summary: Prime Tools does not collect any personal information to use our calculators. All calculations happen in your browser. We use Google Analytics and Google AdSense which may collect anonymised usage data.

1. Who We Are

Prime Tools ("we", "us", "our") operates the website primetoolsapp.com, a free financial calculator platform for users in India and worldwide. Our registered business is based in Pune, Maharashtra, India.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide and improve our services:

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, browser type, device type, and approximate location (city/country level) collected via Google Analytics.
  • Calculator Inputs: Numbers you enter into our calculators are processed entirely in your browser and are never sent to our servers.
  • Cookies: We use cookies for Google Analytics and Google AdSense. See Section 6 for details.
  • Contact Information: If you contact us via email, we retain your email address to respond to your query.

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, phone number, or address unless you voluntarily provide it.
  • We do not store any financial data you enter into our calculators.
  • We do not sell your personal data to third parties.
  • We do not require account creation to use any of our tools.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To understand how users interact with our tools and improve them.
  • To serve relevant advertisements via Google AdSense.
  • To monitor website performance and fix technical issues.
  • To respond to your queries or feedback.

5. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website. Google Analytics collects anonymised data such as pages visited, time on site, and device type. This data is processed by Google LLC. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

6. Google AdSense and Cookies

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website and other websites. You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings.

Types of cookies we use:

  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how you use our site (Google Analytics).
  • Advertising cookies: Used by Google AdSense to show relevant ads.
  • Functional cookies: Remember your preferences on our site.

7. Data Retention

Google Analytics data is retained for 26 months by default. We do not retain any personal data on our own servers beyond email correspondence, which is retained for up to 12 months.

8. Your Rights

Under applicable Indian and international privacy laws, you have the right to:

  • Access information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Opt out of analytics and advertising cookies.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: privacy@primetoolsapp.com

9. Third Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites and encourage you to review their privacy policies.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of our website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
